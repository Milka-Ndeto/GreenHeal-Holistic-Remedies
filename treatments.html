Our Natural Treatments

At GreenHeal, we offer a range of natural treatments and remedies using home-grown herbs, plants, and other organic resources. Below are some of the common ailments we treat and the corresponding natural remedies. Select a category to learn more.

  • Herbal Tea for Cold Relief: A mix of ginger, honey, and lemon to help soothe sore throats and relieve congestion.
  • Lavender for Anxiety: Lavender oil can be used in aromatherapy to reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Turmeric for Inflammation: A powerful anti-inflammatory herb that can be taken in capsule form or as a tea.
  • Burn Relief: Aloe vera gel is a natural remedy for soothing minor burns and sunburns.
  • Cuts & Scrapes: A mixture of honey and turmeric can help disinfect and promote healing of minor cuts and abrasions.
  • Sprains: Ice packs and elevation are crucial in reducing swelling and pain for sprains.
  • Honey for Coughs: A spoonful of honey can help soothe an irritated throat and calm a cough.
  • Ginger for Nausea: Fresh ginger root or ginger tea can help alleviate nausea and upset stomach.
  • Apple Cider Vinegar for Digestion: A tablespoon of apple cider vinegar can help improve digestion and reduce bloating.

We offer personalized consultations to help you determine the best natural treatments for your specific needs. Whether you're looking for remedies for chronic conditions or tips for maintaining overall health, our team is here to guide you.
